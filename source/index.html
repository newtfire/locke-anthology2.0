This project is an effort to construct a digital edition of The New Negro: An Interpretation, edited by Alain Locke (1925). The project is a collaboration between Profs. Bartholomew Brinkman and Elisa Beshero-Bondar and their students at Framingham State University and Penn State Erie, The Behrend College, inspired by their collaboration in the Digital Ethnic Futures Consortium (DEFCon) with thanks to Roopika Risam.
